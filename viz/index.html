    The Boston Social Sphere
    Discourse of Reddit

    This visualization holds over 400,000 Reddit posts and comments about housing, transit, and city life in Boston from 2015 to 2025, grouped by what they discuss and what point of view they take.

    Boston residents

    If you live in Boston, you can use this view to explore what people are discussing, follow how those discussions evolve over time, and see how your own perspective fits within the broader community.

    Government officials

    If you work in city government, this view shows you the dominant topics in community discussion, their relative weight, and how opinions cluster, so you can quickly identify trends and inform policy decisions.

    Thanks to the Metropolitan Area Planning Council (MAPC) for the input that shaped this project.

    How to explore

    Serendipitous discovery

    ✦ s
    Surprise Me — drops you at a random well-supported stance, weighted toward currently-trending subtopics (respects active time range). Globe rotates and the landing point pulses.
    Next surprise
    While a point-of-view card is open from Surprise, use the ✦ next surprise control on the card. The R key sprouts five random posts from what is on screen.
    Click P# pin
    Show a street-interview voice with the nearest pinned Reddit thread.
    Zoom in
    Caption tags fade in on nearby posts.

    Top-down navigation

    Click bars
    Drill topic → subtopic → points of view. Ribbons show hierarchy.
    [ ]
    Cycle points of view within the current subtopic.
    { }
    Cycle subtopics within the current topic.
    Hover breadcrumb
    Linked bar segment highlights.
    URL
    Drilling the nav does not change the address bar. If you open the page with a #cl=… link in the URL, that snapshot is applied once on load.

    Search

    /
    Focus the search box.
    Plain text
    Substring match across subreddits, topics, subtopics, points of view (inc. keywords + descriptions), and phrases.
    Regex
    Type a pattern directly, e.g. rent|mortgage, or use /regex/ / re:pat. Input turns violet. Invalid patterns show an error.
    sub: cl: r/ pos:
    Field scope — restrict a term to one kind, e.g. sub:rent, cl:/housing|afford/, r/boston, pos:shortage (points of view).
    text:
    Search actual post bodies — e.g. text:landlord, text:/(rent|mortgage).*spike/. Hits show snippets with topic context; click jumps to the nearest area.
    -term
    Exclude entries that match the term, e.g. rent -afford. Combines with any operator.
    Multi-token AND
    Space-separated tokens must all match, e.g. cl:housing -tax.
    Focus empty input
    Shows your last 8 searches; Enter reruns.
    Shift+Enter (regex)
    Paint every matching topic/sub on the globe. Chip shows count; × or Esc clears.
    Click r/* suggestion
    Filter the globe to that subreddit (intersects with any drill state).
    Enter
    Search post bodies for the typed text and spotlight matching points. Highlights a count chip; × or Esc clears.
    ↑ ↓ + Enter
    Pick a specific suggestion (topic, subtopic, point of view, snippet) and jump to it instead of running a text search.

    Focus card (bottom-right)

    Breadcrumb pills
    Back to topic or "All topics" with one click.
    Trend badge
    ▲ surging / ▲ trending / ▼ fading — normalized by corpus growth so it's real signal, not a growing-dataset artifact.
    Where it lives
    Top-3 subreddits for this region; click any label to filter the globe.
    Loudest points of view here
    Topic level only — ranked by count × trend; re-ranks by in-range count when the timeline is active. ⇋ marks stances voiced by a different community.

    Point of view card (left panel)

    Evidence posts
    Three real Reddit posts attributed to this stance. Time filter respected.
    Who voiced this stance
    Subreddit breakdown for this point of view; click a label to filter the globe to that community.
    Other stances in this sub
    Sibling points of view; ⇋ marks ones dominated by a different subreddit than the current stance.

    Cross-sub points of view sharing keywords (Jaccard-like), with the same ⇋ cross-community marker.

    Timeline

    t
    Toggle the timeline scrubber.
    Drag
    Click + drag a range; inside an active range, grab the nearer edge.
    ▶ play
    12-month rolling window sweeps through the corpus; any interaction pauses.

    Filter echoes
    Active range paints a band on every sparkline; evidence + Surprise use only in-range posts; agenda and loudest-stances re-rank for the period.

    Filters & state

    Filter chips
    Active subreddit (green) + timeline range (amber) + regex paint (violet) chips appear in the nav header. Each has a × to clear.
    ⟲ Reset x
    Clears drill focus, sub filter, timeline range, regex/text paint, search input, and URL hash — one click (or x) back to fresh.
    ↗ Share
    Copies the current page URL (navigation state is not encoded in the address bar). Use Copy as markdown on the focus card for a link that includes drill and filters.
    Browser back / forward
    Steps through your drill path (topic ▸ sub ▸ stance) one level at a time. Filter changes replace state in place, so scrubbing the timeline or toggling a sub filter doesn't flood history.
    📋 Copy as markdown
    Small button on focus + point-of-view cards copies a pasteable summary (title, trend, voice mix, top points of view / evidence samples, deep-link with current drill + filters + query). For notes or writeups.

    Globe

    Drag
    Rotate the globe.
    Scroll
    Zoom in / out.
    R
    Sample five snippets from posts that are on-screen and match active filters. Esc dismisses the floating cards (or use Reset).
    C (toggle)
    Show connections. Uses the pinned point first, then hovered point, otherwise a visible-thread overview. Press C again or click the connections chip to dismiss.
    Esc
    Clears random cards, connections, pinned node, then bookmarks, search / topic filters / paint, and zoom—one layer at a time. Reset clears everything in one click.
    ← → ↑ ↓
    Push the globe in the indicated direction. Speed scales with zoom.
