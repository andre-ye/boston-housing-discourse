02 — Housing

A city that won't let itself grow

Boston's housing crisis isn't just about price — it's about politics. Neighborhood groups fight almost every new development tooth and nail. The result: one of the lowest housing-supply growth rates of any major American city, and rents that keep climbing anyway.
